HTML Characters

Commonly used characters and codes from the UTF-8 character set, which is used by HTML5 by default.

All characters used in HTML can be inserted using an entity number or entity name.


<p>Â£6 a pint?!</p>

<!-- can also be written as: -->

<p>&#163;6 a pint?!</p>

<!-- or: -->

<p>&pound;6 a pint?!</p>

Aside from the characters with must-use entity codes (&, <, >), if your document is UTF-8, you do not need to use the entity numbers or names listed here — you can plonk these characters directly into your HTML, by copying and pasting, for example.
